ℹ️ Info

PoorNorwegian (PORN) is a meme coin born from one simple truth: Living in Norway is expensive.

We work hard, we earn well — and then the state takes its share. 30% income tax. 25% VAT on almost everything. High prices on fuel, food, and living costs.

PoorNorwegian (PORN) is satire, rebellion, and creativity wrapped into one community-driven token. It represents the everyday Norwegian who feels squeezed by taxes and rising costs — and who starts looking for alternative ways to build wealth.

PORN is not just a joke about being “poor in Norway.” It’s a symbol of financial creativity in a high-tax society.

👥 Who We Are

We’re not scammers. We’re not anonymous bots. We’re not here for a quick rug.

We are two regular guys from Norway who believe in transparency, humor, and the power of real community.

PoorNorwegian (PORN) started as a joke about how expensive life can feel in one of the world’s wealthiest countries. But behind the meme is something real: the belief that people, when united, can create value together.
